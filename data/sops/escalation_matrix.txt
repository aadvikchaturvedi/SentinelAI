SOP: Escalation Matrix
ID: SOP-002

PURPOSE: Define who to contact and when during network incidents.

SEVERITY LEVELS:

LOW (Confidence 70-79%, Single metric anomaly):
  - Action: Monitor and log.
  - Notify: Network Engineer on duty.
  - Timeline: Resolve within 2 hours.

MEDIUM (Confidence 80-89%, Multiple metric anomalies):
  - Action: Begin investigation and prepare rerouting.
  - Notify: Network Lead within 15 minutes.
  - Timeline: Resolve within 1 hour.

HIGH (Confidence 90-94%, Failure worker triggered):
  - Action: Immediate rerouting.
  - Notify: Network Lead immediately, NOC Manager within 10 minutes.
  - Timeline: Resolve within 30 minutes.

CRITICAL (Confidence 95%+, Multiple sites affected):
  - Action: Emergency rerouting + parallel ISP contact.
  - Notify: NOC Manager immediately, CTO within 15 minutes.
  - Timeline: Resolve within 15 minutes or escalate to vendor.

CONTACTS:
  Network Engineer: ext. 2201
  Network Lead: ext. 2202
  NOC Manager: ext. 2200
  ISP NOC: As per ISP contract
