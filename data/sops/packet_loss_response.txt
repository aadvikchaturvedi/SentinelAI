SOP: Packet Loss Response
ID: SOP-001

TRIGGER: Packet loss above 3% on any link.

STEP 1: Classify the severity.
  - 3-5%   → Medium. Monitor for 5 minutes before acting.
  - 5-15%  → High. Begin rerouting immediately.
  - 15%+   → Critical. Escalate and reroute simultaneously.

STEP 2: Notify stakeholders.
  - Medium  → Notify Network Team via internal ticketing.
  - High    → Notify Network Lead within 5 minutes.
  - Critical → Notify NOC Manager and all site managers immediately.

STEP 3: Reroute traffic.
  - Identify alternate tunnel from Graph Engine recommendation.
  - Apply rerouting on hub router.
  - Confirm traffic shift within 3 minutes.

STEP 4: Root cause analysis.
  - Check for physical errors on the interface.
  - Check for upstream ISP issues.
  - Check for QoS misconfiguration causing drops.

STEP 5: Resolution.
  - Restore original path only when packet loss is below 0.5% for 10 minutes.
  - Document incident with timeline and resolution steps.
